Patricio Romano Petronio Alvarez Quintero was a Colombian musician, poet and inspirer of music from the Pacific, who was born in Buenaventura on November 1, 1914. During his childhood, music became an important pillar in his life, which led him to dedicate himself to the guitar before the age of 20.
